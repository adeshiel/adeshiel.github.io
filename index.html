  • B-Essay

    B-Essay


    Have you've ever needed to meet a word count, but never knew what else to write?
    Ever too tired to try and make it up on your own?
    Never heard of the "white-space" trick, like me?
    That's where B-Essay steps in!
    Started in May 2017, B-Essay currently uses Python 2.7 (since Mashape uses 2.7) and Words API to extend your essay for you in many sometimes nonsensensical ways!
